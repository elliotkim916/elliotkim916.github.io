Covid 19 Tracking

With the current pandemic occurring around the world due to Covid-19, I wanted to create an application that could help & inform, & I wanted to do that with my passion for programming. That led me to find an amazing video from the YouTube channel JavaScript Mastery which had an informative tutorial on how to build a Covid 19 Tracking application. This application is able to show the data on the number of infected, recovered, & deaths due to Covid-19 both globally and by individual countries.

Marvel Encyclopedia

Marvel films and television shows are being released more than ever, with new fans constantly on the rise. Therefore, I wanted to create an application that makes it easier for users to search and learn about Marvel characters. This application allows users to search for their favorite Marvel characters and learn about who they are, what comic books they were in, and what events & crossovers they were a part of. Plus, users can save which comic books they've read or want to read in the future.
